Indian Festival

Diwali

Diwali, also known as Deepavali, is one of the most widely celebrated festivals in India.

It is often called the Festival of Lights, symbolizing the victory of light over darkness, good over evil, and knowledge over ignorance.

The festival usually lasts for five days, with each day having its own cultural and spiritual significance

Homes are cleaned and decorated with colorful rangoli, oil lamps (diyas), and beautiful lights.

Families worship Goddess Lakshmi, the deity of wealth and prosperity, praying for happiness and success in the coming year.
